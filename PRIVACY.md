# Privacy Policy — MQTT Scout RN

**Last updated:** June 2026

MQTT Scout RN does not collect, store on our servers, or share any personal data.

- No accounts, analytics, advertising, or tracking.
- Broker hostnames, ports, credentials, and preferred-broker settings are stored **only on your device** (local app storage via AsyncStorage).
- MQTT traffic goes directly between your device and the broker you choose; we do not operate intermediary servers.

If you have questions, open an issue at
https://github.com/mhaberler/rn-mdns-mqtt
